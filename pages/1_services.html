Kindergarten Readiness Services
 
 

SERVICES

Firefly Center provides comprehensive Occupational Therapy intervention for children ages 6 months to 18 years. Our services include:

  • Screening and Evaluation
  • Direct Intervention
  • Consultation (Clinical, home and school)
  • Education
  • Home and school program development and monitoring
  • Environmental Assessment
  • Referral
  • Individual and small group structures

Workshops and lectures to parent, teacher and other professional groups on topics related to OT, child development, Sensory Integration and Processing and Kindergarten readiness are also available.

PROGRAMS

Our therapeutic programs are developed by trained therapists from an extensive and eclectic blend of methods and intervention approaches. They are tailored to each individual child’s strengths and needs, and are designed to maximize skill building and growth. Our programs include:

  • Sensory Integration based therapy
  • Sensory Integration and Praxis Test (SIPT) evaluation
  • Perceptual Enrichment Program (PEP)
  • Handwriting/fine motor intervention (including Handwriting Without Tears)
  • Brain Gym
  • Listening With the Whole Body
  • Sensory Oral Sequential Program for feeding
  • Wilbarger Protocol for sensory defensiveness/Sensory Diets
  • Neuro Developmental Treatment
  • Balance, coordination, motor planning and gross motor work
  • Visual-motor/visual perceptual assistance
  • Movement groups
  • Feeding groups
  • Social skills groups
  • Kindergarten Readiness groups, screening and consultation
